About Us

At Agape Academy International, we are dedicated to nurturing a generation of leaders rooted in Christian values, academic excellence, and a heart for service. Our name, "Agape," reflects our commitment to God’s unconditional love, which serves as the foundation for our mission, education, and community. Our Mission We aim to provide a Christ-centered, academically rigorous education that equips students to grow spiritually, intellectually, and socially. We prepare students to be compassionate, well-rounded individuals who make a positive impact on the world. Core Values Faith: We instill a deep understanding of God’s Word, guiding students to develop a personal relationship with Jesus Christ and integrate their faith into all areas of life. Excellence: Our academic programs are designed to challenge students and foster a love of learning, critical thinking, and creativity. Service: We encourage our students to engage in service projects that make a difference in their communities and the world, reflecting Christ’s love in action. Community: We cultivate a welcoming environment where students, families, and staff support one another, fostering a spirit of unity and belonging. Our Approach Agape Academy International offers a comprehensive educational experience that combines a strong academic curriculum with biblical teachings. Our educators are dedicated to mentoring and inspiring students to discover their God-given potential and to lead with integrity and purpose. We invite you to become part of our Agape Academy International family, where faith, learning, and love come together to shape tomorrow’s leaders.
